Sri Lanka See Off Hong Kong in Abu Dhabi — Match Report

Men's T20 Asia Cup 2025 — 8th Match, Group B

Published: Sep 15, 2025 · estimated reading: 4–6 minutes

Quick result

Sri Lanka chased down Hong Kong's total to secure a win in their Group B encounter. The chase was tidy — Sri Lanka paced the innings efficiently and wrapped up the match with overs to spare, showing the experience advantage over the associate side.

What happened (brief)

Hong Kong posted a competitive total after some energetic lower-order hitting, but Sri Lanka’s top-order and a steady middle order took control during the chase. A few tidy overs from Sri Lanka’s bowlers kept the required rate manageable and negated Hong Kong’s big-hitting late in the innings.

Key performers

  • Sri Lanka batter — anchored the chase and provided a calm hand at the crease.
  • Hong Kong quicks — produced good death over hitting but lacked consistent breakthroughs.
  • Fielding moments — Sri Lanka’s fielding saved crucial singles and put pressure on the chase.

Takeaways

The match highlighted the gulf in experience: Sri Lanka’s game management in the chase contrasted with Hong Kong’s brilliance in patches but lack of sustained pressure. For Sri Lanka it was a businesslike win; for Hong Kong, several positives to build on had they maintain control in the powerplay and middle overs.
