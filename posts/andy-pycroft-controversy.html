PCB Demands Immediate Removal of Match Referee Andy Pycroft

Explainer: the handshake controversy, PCB reaction and ICC’s likely response

What triggered the row?

After the India-Pakistan match, a handshake incident and subsequent claims about instructions given by the match referee sparked a formal complaint from the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB). The PCB has demanded the immediate removal of Andy Pycroft and lodged a complaint with the ICC alleging inappropriate conduct surrounding the incident.

How the ICC might react

According to reporting, the ICC is likely to review the complaint but may not accede to immediate replacement — procedural checks and the referee’s own report will influence any decision. Quick decisions are rare because of governance and the need for due process.

Implications

The dispute escalates tensions around high-profile fixtures and highlights how off-field incidents can quickly inflame tournament relations. For fans and administrators, the episode raises questions about match protocols, neutral officiating and communication after contentious moments.
