Europe’s Elite Back in Action — Champions League on Stan Sport

Matchday 1 previews and how Stan Sport is covering the competition.

What to expect

The Champions League kickoff brings heavyweight fixtures — title-holders and perennial favourites face tough opponents in matchday one. With the expanded format, fans have more games and more storylines early in the season.

Stan Sport coverage

In Australia, Stan Sport has become the home for every match — live, on demand and in some cases 4K replays and extras. If you’re in Stan’s region, the schedule brings big matchups such as Liverpool vs Atlético and other top clashes.

Why it matters

The new format and broadcasting deals reshuffle how fans access matches worldwide. The return offers a feast of football and sets the tone for who looks strongest early on.
